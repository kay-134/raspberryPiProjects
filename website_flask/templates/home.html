Mission Statement

Climate Science Communication for Youth is a student and womxn of color led initiative that seeks to bridge the gap between climate advocacy and STEAM communities. Our goal is to create an inclusive outlet for members to engage in dialogue and civics, and to empower Black, Indigenous, and youth of color. To rebuild flawed institutions, our mission is to provide the resources for BIPOC, particularly womxn of color, to thrive in political spaces through advocacy training and personal support.
